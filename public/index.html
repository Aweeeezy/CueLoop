Queue Loop

Have you ever been on a road trip or at a party where somebody won't give up control of the music selection? Are you tired of standing up to change the song on the device plugged into the speakers?

Queue Loop is a web app for conveniently distributing the blessing (or burden) of choosing the next song. Just create a new DJ booth, invite some friends (or tell them to find your booth by name) and start queuing your favorite songs from YouTube.
