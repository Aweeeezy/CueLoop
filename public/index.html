QLoop

Have you ever been on a road trip or at a party and somebody won't forfeit control of the music selection? Are you tired of having to stand up and walk to the device plugged into your speakers in order to change the song?

QLoop is a web app for conveniently distributing the blessing (or burden) of choosing the next song. Just create a new DJ booth, invite some friends via email, or simply tell them to find your booth by name, and start queuing your favorite songs from YouTube.
